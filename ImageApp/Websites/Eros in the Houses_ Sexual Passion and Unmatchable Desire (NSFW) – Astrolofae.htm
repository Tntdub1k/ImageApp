Eros in the Houses: Sexual Passion and Unmatchable Desire (NSFW)

The asteroid Eros relates to the god of sex and desire in Greek mythology, and thus in astrology comes to show us our own sexuality (habits and desires, not orientation). However, Eros isn’t just sexual (though this is one of it’s main representations), it is also desire and passion as a whole: Eros can show us what we’d do when consumed with love!

Whilst there is a lot of information out there about Eros in the signs, little is written about Eros in the houses, which is why this article is going to address exactly that. If you wish to learn about Eros in the signs, use the links above! Here is a calculator for your Eros sign, and to insert it into a natal chart, use Astro.com (guide here), simply type ‘433’ into the asteroid selection bar.

Eros in the first house: Passion and desire are key to the being of this native. Their first thought is to do anything for their partner, even if the partner is purely a sexual one. Especially when in conjunction to the Ascendent, this native glows with passion for life and love, seeing that no less than perfect is good enough. There may be a selfish aspect to them in sexual terms, but mostly the flare is active and energetic. Sexual passion is something they just know!

Eros in the second house: The second house has strong links to values and materialism, and for this reason Eros in the second house may show itself via giving. They enjoy buying gifts for their partners to show off their passion, and may be more inclined to use objects in the bedroom. These natives have strong values of how they think sexual encounters should be, and may be secure in the kinks they have. As well as this, Eros in the second house may have a certain view on how commitment should be shown, as the desire of Eros takes on the value of the second house.

Eros in the third house: Speaking their mind, people with Eros in the third house prefer communication mixed in with passion and have no problem talking about it. Intellectual stimulation is all part of the foreplay for them, and a desirable partner – both sexual and romantic – should be able to converse with them on a deep level. They express their strong desire through words, whether this be a text, a poem, or a whisper in your ear. These people are loud under the sheets and will let you know what they want.

Eros in the fourth house: Eros in the fourth house may be regarded as shy by some, but actually Eros is considered most at home in the element of water! Being a hater house, Eros’ undying passion is allowed to flood through the native’s emotions, making them feel the desire they show to you. This placement may prefer sexual encounters to be close, meaningful, and in the privacy of the home. Sexual partners are often close to them and not just fleeting ones.

Eros in the fifth house: Eros’ desire is shining in the fifth house, as the fifth house relates to expression and sex! Eros embraces the spotlight of the fifth house, allowing it’s desire to be known and be held to no boundaries. These people may wish to be the best sexual partner you’ve had, and to leave a lasting memory in your sheets. Eros placed here wants fun encounters with an ounce of competition, so they can truly let their passion run free with you.

Eros in the sixth house: Dutiful, a lover with Eros in the sixth house wants to make sure they do everything exactly as you love. Passion isn’t limited to just the sheets with this lover, as they also want to show you they desire you through helping you in day to day life! Under the sheets, Eros in the sixth house forgets about themselves and focuses solely on their lover, as doing a good job is what turns them on. They may the type to keep it in the home, but that doesn’t limit them to the bedroom!

Eros in the seventh house: Their passion is best expressed in one to one relationships, possibly with a partner rather than just a sexual encounter. Their desire is shown through concentrating on their partner and the relationship between you and them. These people may prefer to have one partner in the bedroom rather than multiple, and thus could be interested in friends with benefits, or not having sex outside of relationships at all. Whichever they choose, it has a personal touch.

Eros in the eighth house: Desire is like Chinese whispers: Secret and yet passed on. The eighth house shrouds this persons desire in secrecy, but by no means does this mean that sexual passion is entirely private for them. The eighth house regards shared resources, meaning that these people may be okay with multiple sexual partners or situations. They may also try more risky sexual situations, as their desires can be taboo at times. To sum up, desire is a risk you play in the privacy of a glass box.

Eros in the ninth house: The ninth house is typically concerned with travel, philosophy and further education, so it may be likely that this person has deep questions and understandings of sexual relationships and expressions of desire. They’re the kind to walk a million miles to show how much they value you, and also may fall into sexual relationships on travels. Long distance relationships for them are still filled with the sexual passion of close ones.

Eros in the tenth house: These people have an aura of desirable passion. Their public image reflects a certain degree of sexuality, and they may even get their kicks from knowing this. For some, there is no reason sexual encounters need to be kept between four walls. For Eros in the tenth house, appearing desirable is a sign of power, and that power is like their war paint. They may be more uncomfortable with their sexual desires then others, and may be open to conversing on the topic.

Eros in the eleventh house: Eros in this house believes desire is to be shared, and there is no reason to keep the passion between just two people. Their desire leaks out and inspires their communities, even when not in a sexual manner. They may enjoy taking sexual encounters outside the bedroom, or engaging with more than one individual at once. They are also very open to trying new things and change under the sheets, as their desire is innovative and changing.

Eros in the twelfth house: This placement may dream and fantasise of their different desires often, but ultimately they prefer the comfort of four walls when releasing their passions. There’s a magical element of desire for them, as desire is not just a light subject, it is an unmatchable love. These people may be the last you think of when it comes to sexual desire as they feel no need to advertise it, but that is not to say they lack passion. Eros in the twelfth house holds passion like a photo in a locket necklace.
