Nibiru Delineation

Nibiru seems to have to do with the perception of the galactic center of all its distal entities. It is the GC considering the small planet Earth.

The relationship between the huge GC and the small and meaningless earth... Meekness seems to be a Key to understanding Nibiru especially in the 6th house. 


Nibiru in the houses:


1st - Rough start, awkward beginnings, much is still unknown, unfathomable, formless. Nothing exists, no personalities yet, where is everything/everybody?Pre-birth, umbilical cord still connected


2nd - Slow development


3rd - Detriment or fall, long breaks..


4th - Plentiful resources


5th - Many available opportunities


6th - Meekness as a Key to Nibiru


7th - Fall or detriment


8th - Sending sparks from the galactic center to the planet Earth, starseed missionaries, unknown possible discoveries await you.


9th - The tree truck supplying branches with water and nutrients, ask and you shall receive


10th - The GC has other concerns than the small planet, may send money in the mail, can be bribed.


11th - In good company, many other places with similar scenarios, ready friends wanting to offer understanding and (creative) solutions to your earth problem


12th - Exalted, all will be one in the end, mother and daughter repairing their separation
